Peanut Butter and Jelly

This dish is said to be the best lasagna that has ever be prepared.

The lasagna recipe you are to see was passed down from the Godfather himself. Legends says it is the best recipes ever. It takes over 3 hours to prepare, cook, and serve. It feeds 12 poeple. and is best served on the finest china, with a peice of homemade garlic bread. Zues himself would crumble under the immense power of the lasanga.

Below listed are some ingredients and steps to follow in order to made the family meal.

  • Peanut Butter. Chunky for quantity, creamy for quality
  • Loaf of Bread
  • Jelly or Jam
Follow these steps to achieve food GOD!
  1. Get a couple slices of bread from the bread drawer or cabinet. Grab a knife from the drawer, jelly from the fridge, and peanut butter from the canned goods cupboard. Unless you're a menace to society and keep it in the fridge?!?!?!
  2. Spread a vast amount of preanut butter and jelly on both slabs of bread. Smush both side together until the contents are oozing out. Lick around edges of crust to ensure nothing is wasted.
  3. Once excess is cleaned and you have carefully licked the knife to removed unsed product. Cut into triangles and enjoy. Or eat it whole, like a normal adult person. I always like carrot sticks or chips with mine.
