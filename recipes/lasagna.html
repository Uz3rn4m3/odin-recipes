Lasagna

This dish is said to be the best lasagna that has ever be prepared.

The lasagna recipe you are to see was passed down from the Godfather himself. Legends says it is the best recipes ever. It takes over 3 hours to prepare, cook, and serve. It feeds 12 poeple. and is best served on the finest china, with a peice of homemade garlic bread. Zues himself would crumble under the immense power of the lasanga.

Below listed are some ingredients and steps to follow in order to made the family meal.

  • 1 lb Sweet Italian Sausage
  • 3/4 pound minced beef
  • one half cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Follow these steps to achieve greatness
  1. Cook beef, sausage, and onions until golden brown. Then mix tomatoe paste, sause, and crushed. Also, throw in the water too. season with with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons parsley. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  2. Bring a bit pot of water to a boil. Cook lasagna for 8 to 10 min. While you are waiting for the water to boil and noodles to cook. Begin crafting the ricotta cheese mixture. combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt into a mixing bowl.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  4. Now it is time to layer! First spread the sause on the bottom on the pan. It should be 1 1/2 cups thick. Then layer on noodles. 6 should be plenty and spread half of the ricotta cheese mix on top. Finally, layer on a third of mozzarella cheese slices. Repeat this process until the pan is full. Cover with foil and back for 25 min.
  5. After first baking cycle, take off foil and bake for another 25 min. Let cool for 15 min and serve.
